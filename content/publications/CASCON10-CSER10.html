Abstract

This exhibit demonstrates a tool that fixes concurrency bugs automatically using genetic programming. A bug fix is formed, by iteratively applying concurrency mutations and determining if the mutations have improved the quality of the program. The concurrency mutations are based on common fixes for concurrency bugs like deadlocks and data races.
